Good Will Hunting

Robin Williams played a therapist for the main character Will Hunting in the movie Good Will Hunting. This character is one of his most iconic roles, as he coaches the main character with wisdom and warmth. Williams obtained an Academy Award in 1998 due to his acting as a supporting role.


An impactful quote from this movie that obtained 61 award nominations is "You don't know about real loss, 'cause it only occurs when you've loved something more than you love yourself. And I doubt you've ever dared to love anybody that much."
